# Finance

## Financial Reporting and Analysis Features

### Sales Reporting

- **Sales Report:** Provides revenue tracking on a daily, weekly, and monthly basis to monitor sales trends and performance.

### Payment Collection and Credit Analysis

- **Payment Collection:** Tracks customer payment health, including payment aging, outstanding amounts, and credit scoring to assess risk and improve cash flow management.
- **Credit Note / Return / Refund:** Monitors all credit notes, product returns, and refunds to ensure accurate financial reconciliation.

### Profitability and Financial Statement Analysis

- **Profit and Loss (P&L) Analysis:** Includes regular monthly balance sheet analysis to evaluate overall financial health and profitability.
- **Cost Tracking:** Monitors major cost categories such as Cost of Goods Sold (COGS), operating expenses (OPEX), payroll costs, electricity, packing materials, and identifies the top 10 expenses.

### Margin and Trend Analysis

- **Customer Profit Margin Report:** Tracks profit margin trends by customer to identify high-value relationships and optimize pricing strategies.
- **Supplier Profit Margin Report:** Analyzes profit margin trends for suppliers, supporting negotiation and procurement strategies.


### Advanced Filtering Options

- **Filter Capabilities:** Allows users to filter data by outlet, time range, product weight, customer, product country, product brand, product variety, and supplier, enabling targeted analysis and reporting.
